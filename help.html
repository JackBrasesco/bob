Help

Web
------------------------

LOOKUP/SEARCH: Asking Bob to 'lookup' or 'search' something will open a google search for that thing in a new tab.
Add 'on youtube' or 'youtube search' to your query to find your result on youtube.
FIND: Use the command 'find' to find nearby resturants or attractions. (i.e. find pizza nearby).
GO TO: Want to go directly to a website? Just say 'go to' and then the website url, or for 'youtube', 'drive', and 'chat' just say those keywords
DEFINE/SYNONYM: Need to quickly check the definition of a word? use the command 'define' plus the word you are looking for. also ask bob to look for a 'synonym'
CHECK EMAIL: Type 'check email' for a quick look at your email

Math
------------------------

MATH: Bob can also do math! Just say the operation 'multiply','divide','add', or 'divide' and then the problem '3*4' or '3 and 4'
use 'find the square root of __' to find the square root of _____!

Timing
------------------------

SET A TIMER/ALARM: Need to keep track of the time? Ask bob to 'set a timer' for however long you need!
Need to be reminded when you have that important meeting for work? Ask bob to 'set an alarm' for (hour) (am/pm) (set an alarm for 7 pm)

Conversation
------------------------

Bob also has conversational features, ask him anything or talk with him about life for hours!
